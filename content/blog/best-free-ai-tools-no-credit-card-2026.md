# Best Free AI Tools in 2026 (No Credit Card Needed)

You don’t need to pay—or even add a card—to try most of the best AI tools. Here’s a short list of solid free options in 2026.

---

## No signup or card required

**ChatGPT (free)** — Create an account with email; no card. You get the default model with rate limits. Enough for learning and light use.

**Claude (free)** — Same idea: free tier with limits. Good for writing and analysis without paying.

**Google Gemini** — Free tier via Google account. Handy if you’re already in the Google ecosystem.

---

## Free tier (account, no card)

**Copy.ai** — Free tier with a monthly word allowance. Good for short-form copy and trying the product.

**Grammarly** — Free browser extension for grammar and clarity; no card for the basic tier.

**Replit AI** — Free tier for coding in the browser. Good for learning and small projects.

**Amazon CodeWhisperer** — Free for individual developers. Completions and security scanning.

---

## How to choose

- **General chat and writing** → ChatGPT or Claude free  
- **Short copy and marketing** → Copy.ai  
- **Grammar and tone** → Grammarly  
- **Coding** → Replit AI or CodeWhisperer
